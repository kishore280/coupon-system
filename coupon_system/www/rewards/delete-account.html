Delete Your Oxifix Account

This page lets users of the Oxifix app (published by Oxifix Retail Pvt Ltd) request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to request deletion

Send an email to support@oxifix.in from the email or phone number registered with your account, with the subject "Delete my Oxifix account". Include your registered mobile number so we can verify your identity.

To confirm ownership, we may send a one-time code (OTP) to your registered mobile number before processing the request.

What gets deleted

When your request is processed, we delete your account and its associated personal data, including:

  • Your name and registered mobile number
  • Your loyalty points balance and points transaction history
  • For Sales Partners: your KYC details (PAN, Aadhaar) and bank/payout details

Some records may be retained where required by law (for example, tax and financial records for completed transactions) for the legally mandated period, after which they are deleted.

Processing time

Requests are processed within 30 days of verification.
